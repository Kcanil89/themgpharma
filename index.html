Project Introduction

Our Vision: Advancing Healthcare in Nepal

Mountain Glacier Pharmaceuticals is establishing a state-of-the-art facility in Chapur-2, Judibela, Rautahat (2.9 Km from East-Wast Highway) for the production of high-quality Sterile Parenteral Products. Our commitment is to enhance Nepal's self-reliance in essential medicines and explore global export opportunities.
